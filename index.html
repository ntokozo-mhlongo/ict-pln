ICT Professional Learning Network

Welcome! I am Miss.Mhlongo. This space curates my professional learning network (PLN) across platforms. A PLN is the purposeful web of people, communities, tools, and resources we learn with and from. In the ICT field, a strong PLN accelerates skill growth, exposes you to new pedagogies, and keeps you current with rapid technological change.
